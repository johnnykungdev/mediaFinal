About

"I only prepare food with the freshest ingredients and serve them with passion"

- Aunt Francis

Francis Murray had a dream. She wanted the people she loved to taste all of the delicious foods that she had as a child. Her grandmother was a wonderful cook and taught Francis her way around the kitchen.

After her grandmother passed away, Francis continued to cherish all of their memories together through her love of cooking. After nearly a decade of cooking for her own family and friends, Francis set off to share her talents with the world.

Francis Murray and her dog, Baguette, opened a food truck in 2012 called, Aunt Francis. It became such a huge hit that the food truck began to travel the world. Aunt Francis has become a pioneer for other food trucks that serve some seriously delicious homestyle cooking.
